Design Thinking for Blockchains is a one-day workshop for exploring
decentralization opportunities in any domain in a creative and collaborative way.

Who is it for?

We designed the workshop for anyone who is interested in getting creative with decentralization as a system design exercise. The more diverse backgrounds, the better.

Why does it matter?

Considering blockchains will have a significant impact on all walks of life, protocol design should be a multi-disciplinary activity and include a variety of inputs in the conception stage to prevent creator biases baked in platforms.
